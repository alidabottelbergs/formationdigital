My experience

At first, the content and the professor's accent were a little confusing. But in the end, it was one of the most interesting courses thanks to the practical cases. I now understand how my home alarm works. It's all about the sensor, code and language to give the right commands and interpret the results into action.

Arduino

To learn and experiment with sensors, we got an Arduino starter kit. This provided us a practical and fun way to learn to understand how electrics works.
