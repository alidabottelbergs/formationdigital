About the program

"Through this interuniversity lifelong learning program, we aim to train you in both technical and transversal digital and IT skills and to reward you with a certification upon successful completion. The program was designed to increase diversity in IT related work-environments, to support competitiveness, employability, and social innovation. We strive to achieve this through collaboration and by adapting our training programs to the evolution of professional practices, to institutional strategies, and to the needs of the participants.""
