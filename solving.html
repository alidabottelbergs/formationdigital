Applications & perspectives: solving real world issues with IT & digital

By Marie-Mo and guest speakers

Course content

A series of guest speakers will demonstrate the diversity of applications and perspectives related to IT:

- Research data analysis

- Client relation management systems

- Student information systems

- Learning Management systems

- Soft skills in IT management

- Kidney function modeling: from the bench to the bed side

- Data interoperability

- Climate modelling

Personal experience

The purpose of this module is promising, but the implementation could be better. It would be interesting to see how people with little to no IT background are working in a technology department. This can be inspiring for the participants on how they can grow in the technology sector. The lectures we had were sometimes too technical, because the people who are applying IT into their work and gave the lectures had a technical background. Other lectures with less technical details, could also have been a topic in the module Strategy and Governance. There was also the opportunity to have one of the women part of the InspiringFifty Belgium as a mentor. After subscribing for this mentorship, we haven’t heard back from the teacher until today. So in short, a lot of potential for this module to have added value to the course but the potential is not yet reached. Since this was the ‘first edition’, I am convinced that they will take the feedback into account for the next edition.
